Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026 · App: 8 Doktor (8 Doctors)

This app does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services.

English

Overview

8 Doktor (“we”, “the app”) is a gentle daily wellness coaching app. We designed it so your progress stays on your device by default. We do not operate our own user accounts or backend servers for your activity data.

Data stored on your device

  • Daily activity for the eight wellness modules (water, sunlight, meditation, sleep, nature, laughter, exercise, nutrition)
  • App preferences (language, reminder times, onboarding state)
  • Premium gratitude journal entries (if you use that feature)

This data is stored locally using Apple’s SwiftData framework on your iPhone or iPad.

Apple Health (optional)

If you grant permission, the app may read or write selected HealthKit data (for example steps, sleep, or water intake) to sync your progress. Health data is processed according to Apple’s HealthKit policies and remains under your control in the Health app. You can revoke access at any time in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.

Notifications (optional)

If you enable gentle reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. We do not send push notifications from our own servers.

Subscriptions

Premium purchases are processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card details. Subscription status is verified on-device through Apple’s App Store APIs.

Data we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers for cross-app tracking
  • No sale of personal data to third parties
  • No user account registration required to use the app
  • No analytics SDK that uploads your wellness entries to our servers

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@eightdoctors.app

Genel bakış

8 Doktor, nazik bir günlük wellness koçluk uygulamasıdır. İlerlemeniz varsayılan olarak cihazınızda kalacak şekilde tasarlanmıştır. Aktivite verileriniz için kendi sunucularımızda kullanıcı hesabı işletmiyoruz.

Cihazda saklanan veriler

  • Sekiz wellness modülüne ait günlük kayıtlar
  • Uygulama tercihleri (dil, hatırlatma saatleri, onboarding)
  • Premium minnettarlık günlüğü girdileri (kullanıyorsanız)

Bu veriler SwiftData ile yalnızca cihazınızda saklanır.

Apple Sağlık (isteğe bağlı)

İzin verirseniz seçili HealthKit verileri okunabilir veya yazılabilir. Erişimi Ayarlar → Sağlık üzerinden istediğiniz zaman kapatabilirsiniz.

Abonelikler
